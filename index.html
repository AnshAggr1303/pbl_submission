Core Features
📷
OCR Bill Scanner
Tesseract.js + Gemini Vision. Photo → items extracted → stock updated. Handles Indian supplier bill formats.
📖
Recipe Engine
Define once. Sell a dish — all ingredients deduct atomically. Sufficiency check before every write. Stock never goes negative.
🤖
3 AI Agents
Reorder, Shrinkage Detective, Demand Forecaster. All proposals require human approval. Vercel Cron scheduled.
💬
Natural Language Co-pilot
Ask anything about inventory in plain English. Gemini Flash + rule-based fallbacks when keys unavailable.
